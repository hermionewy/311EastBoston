Understand Easties
from Bos: 311

Boston residents could call 311, Mayor's office, anytime request for non-emergency city services and information.

There are 12964 requests for non-emergency city service through Bos:311 in East Boston in 2017. The issues mentioned the most include street cleaning, highway maintenance, sanitation, traffic, building, trees, street lights, recycling, housing and animals.

This map randomly displays the 311 requests related to East Boston in 2017.

12:00 AM, 2017

Enforcement & abandoned vehicles is one of the most frequent complaints about issues by the East Boston residents. In 2017, 2313 requests are related to it.

Abandoned vehicles are safety hazards and they blight neighborhoods. Among all neighborhoods, Eagle Hill has seen the most complaints.

Judging from the 311 requests, street cleaning is the second most complained issue by the East Boston residents. In 2017, 1671 requests are related it. Eagle Hill and Maverick has sent more requests than Orient Heights.

Recycling and Sanitation service requests were also popular. Orient Heights residents showed more passion for these services than others.

There are 1388 requests for Highway Maintenance, which took up more than 10% of all requests.
